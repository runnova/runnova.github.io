Meet Nova OS

NovaOS is the superpower for any device.

Nova OS is a customizable, web desktop system designed for any device with a browser. It offers a fast, secure, and feature-rich experience for gaming and daily tasks, with dynamic personalization and seamless updates.

Frequenty Asked Questions

Is NovaOS free and open-source?
chevron_right
Yes, NovaOS is Free and Open Source. But without community contributions.
What licenses can NovaOS apps have?
chevron_right
They can operate in any licences. NovaOS apps are local and undistributed. Once they are installed or created, they follow the license specified inside them. If unspecified, they follow the MIT license.
Where does NovaOS save the files?
chevron_right
NovaOS, by default, is totally local. It doesn't store any data online. This provides immense security advantages. All files are encrypted (AES symmetric key encryption), compressed (lossless) and get saved efficiently in the browser IndexedDB.
Is NovaOS a linux distro?
chevron_right
NovaOS Web is not a linux distro. It a web desktop system that runs on your browser without being your operating system. It an abstract virtual experience designed for a fast, secure, and feature-rich experience for gaming and daily tasks, with dynamic personalization and seamless updates.
Is my files secure in NovaOS?
chevron_right
NovaOS uses the AES-GSM encryption method to locally encrypt every single one of your files. After you set a password, all your files are safely encrypted and stored in your browser. Making it impossibly hard to decrypt them if you used a diverse and long password.
